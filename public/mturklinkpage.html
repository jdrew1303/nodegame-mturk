Your Task

  • Purpose: Scientific experiment to understand how individuals interact and make decisions in groups.
  • What you will do: If you decide to participate, you will play an interactive game with other human participants. You might need to wait a short while until all participants have joined the experiment.
  • Requirements:
    1. You must be 18 years old or above,
    2. You must have not played a HIT called "Artex" before,
    3. Your browser must accept cookies and have JavaScript enabled,
    4. Only the browsers Chrome >= 20, Safari >= 6, Firefox >= 20, IE >= 10, Opera >= 12 are allowed to play,
    5. Due to the special game interface, you cannot participate from tablets or mobile phones.
    6. Due to the special game interface, your screen must have a resolution of 1366x768px or more.
  • Time required: Approximately 25 to 35 minutes.
  • Risks: There are no anticipated risks associated with participating in this study and your participation will remain anonymous.
  • Compensation: You will receive a variable bonus for participating. The exact amount depends on your choices and the choices of other participants in the game. We expect the bonus to vary between 2 and 4 dollars.
  • Trouble Ticket: In case of any technical issues, or a delay with the payment please search for a HIT called "Artex Trouble Ticket" and report your issues there.
